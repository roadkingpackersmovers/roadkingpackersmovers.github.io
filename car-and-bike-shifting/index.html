    Car and Bike Shifting

    Welcome to RoadKing Packers and Movers, your trusted companion for hassle-free vehicle relocation services across India. With a legacy of excellence and a commitment to reliability, we strive to make your car, bike, or scooty shifting experience seamless and stress-free. At RoadKing, we understand the importance of your vehicles, and our dedicated team is here to ensure their safe and timely delivery to your desired destination.

      Contact

    What to expect while Car/Bike/Scooty Shifting

    1. Initial Assessment: Our vehicle relocation process begins with a thorough assessment of your relocation needs. Whether you’re moving a single car, bike, or scooty, or an entire fleet, our team works closely with you to understand your requirements and tailor our services accordingly.

    2. Customized Packing: Once we have assessed your needs, we proceed with customized packing solutions designed to protect your vehicles during transit. Our experienced packers utilize high-quality packing materials and advanced techniques to safeguard your vehicles from scratches, dents, and other damage during transportation.

    3. Secure Loading and Transportation: With safety as our top priority, our trained professionals handle the loading of your vehicles onto specialized carriers with utmost care. We employ secure tie-downs and advanced loading techniques to ensure that your vehicles are securely fastened and protected throughout the journey.

    4. Efficient Transit Management: During transit, our dedicated logistics team monitors the progress of your vehicle relocation in real-time. We leverage advanced tracking technology to provide you with regular updates on the status of your shipment, ensuring transparency and peace of mind throughout the process.

    5. Timely Delivery: Upon reaching the destination, our team coordinates the unloading and delivery of your vehicles with precision and efficiency. Whether you’re relocating within the city or across state borders, we strive to deliver your vehicles on time, allowing you to resume your routine without delay.

    Steps for easy shifting with us

    At RoadKing Packers and Movers, we follow a detailed 10-step approach, to meet customer needs and expectations.

    • Initial Assessment

      We conduct a thorough pre-shifting assessment to understand your needs, plan accordingly and discuss prices.

    • Moving Strategy

      We tailor a plan based on your requirements for house shifting, office shifting or car/bike shifting, and address all concerns.

    • Professional Packing

      Our skilled team packs your items carefully using high-quality waterproof materials.

    • Detailed Labeling

      Each item is labeled, and a report is created that lists all items. One copy shared to customer.

    • Efficient Loading

      Trained movers load items onto vehicles with precision and modern equipment.

    • Safe Transportation

      Well-maintained vehicles and experienced drivers ensure secure transport.

    • Methodical Unloading

      Belongings are unloaded and placed in designated rooms as instructed.

    • Professional Unpacking and Assembly

      Optional service includes unpacking, furniture setup, and reassembly.

    • Thorough Quality Inspection

      We check items to ensure intact delivery and meet your satisfaction.

    • Continued Support

      Post-move assistance is available to address any additional needs or concerns.

    Things to Take Care

    1. Documentation : Ensure that all necessary documentation, including vehicle registration papers, insurance certificates, and identification proofs, are in order before the relocation process begins.
    2. Vehicle Preparation: Prior to relocation, remove any personal belongings from your vehicles and ensure that they are in good working condition for transit.
    3. Clear Communication: Maintain open communication with our team throughout the relocation process to address any concerns or queries promptly.

    Do’s and Don’ts

    Its always good to consider few do’s and dont’s to ensure easier and faster shifting.

    Do’s

    • Do inform us about any specific requirements or special considerations related to your vehicle relocation.
    • Do conduct a thorough inspection of your vehicles before and after transit to ensure their condition.
    • Do provide accurate contact information to facilitate smooth communication during the relocation process.

    Dont’s

    • Don’t overlook the importance of proper packing and securing of your vehicles for transit.
    • Don’t delay in providing necessary documentation and information required for the relocation process.
    • Don’t hesitate to reach out to our team in case of any issues or emergencies during transit.
