    FAQ

    1. What services does RoadKing Packers and Movers offer?

    We offer house shifting, office shifting, car and bike shifting across all over India.

    2. How do I request a quote for house/office/car/bike shifting?

    You can call us at 9818073792 or fill out the form using below button

      Get Quote

    3. Can I schedule an appointment for an in-home survey before booking?

    Yes we conduct a survey to give you best estimate and do strategic planning for your shifting.

      Contact

    4. What factors determine the cost of shifting?

    The cost of house shifting depends on majorly two factors :

    • Distance of delivery location from pickup location
    • Quantity and weight of items

    5. How much advance notice do I need to provide for booking shifting services?

    We can provide services on same day you reach out to us. Thanks to our massive fleet of 450+ vehicles, our time to action is as low as an hour.

    6. Does RoadKing Packers and Movers offer packing services for fragile items?

    Yes, we provide bubble wrap with an additional cushion wrap for extra safety. Plus the fragile items are packed in special boxes, marked with yellow tapes to help identify them while picking, so that extra care can be given to these items, while loading/unloading.

    7. How are heavy or bulky items handled during house/office/bike shifting?

    Our team of experts are trained to carefully handle all your belongings. Almost all household items can be man held and done by a team of specialists. For bulkier items we use fork-lift.

    8. Does RoadKing Packers and Movers offer insurance coverage for household goods?

    Yes, you can opt for insurance while booking with us. Please mention to our representative while booking

      Contact

    9. Can I request special handling for sensitive office equipment during the relocation process?

    Yes. Though our packing experts, by default, identify fragile items and label them separately, you can explicitly request for other items to be identified as fragile.

    10. Does RoadKing Packers and Movers provide packing materials for office relocation?

    Yes. We provide boxes, bubble wrap, cardboard wrap, foam padding and packing peanuts to ensure quality packaging.

    11. Are there any restrictions on the types of vehicles RoadKing Packers and Movers can transport?

    We support all 2-wheeler and 4-wheeler transporation, across all over India.

    12. Can I include personal items in my car or bike during transport?

    We encourage you to remove all your personal belongings from car and keep them with you, or, get them packaged separately with household items if that is also being done.

    13. What documentation do I need to provide for car/bike shifting?

    We need your self-attested copy of ID proof and RC of the vehicle.

    14. Do you provide labour to carry items?

    Yes, we cover everything from packing, loading, transportation and unloading. You just need to book a delivery with us and on pickup day, just guide our team what needs to be packaged. Rest they will take care of everything else.
